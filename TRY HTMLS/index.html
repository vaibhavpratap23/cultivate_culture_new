Our Mission

Cultivate Culture bridges the gap between international travelers seeking authentic farming experiences and Indian farmers with land to share. We create meaningful cultural exchanges while supporting sustainable agriculture and rural economies.

Founded in 2020, we've connected over 1,200 visitors with farming communities across 15 Indian states, generating supplemental income for farmers while preserving traditional agricultural knowledge.
